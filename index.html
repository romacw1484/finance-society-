The Fordham Finance Society's mission is to promote knowledge, provide information, develop networks, and prepare students to develop into ethical and professional members of the financial community. The society accomplishes these goals by organizing informational events, training workshops, alumni meet-and-greets, and informal club meetings, as well as disseminating relevant financial news and updates.

- FFS Executive Board
